*In this project, ecological niche modeling was carried out for the lynx lynx species using R programming and fundamentally biomod2 package. 
*The current and future distribution projections of the species are included in the modeling. 
*Occurrence data of the species were obtained from GBIF website, distribution map from IUCN website, and current and future bioclimatic data from Worldclim's website. 
*Data could not be uploaded due to their large size.
